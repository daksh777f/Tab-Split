# 3-Minute Live Demo Script: Tab Split

**Goal**: Prove that Tab Split accurately resolves a multi-party dispute using a real-time, cloud-managed knowledge graph.
**Time Limit**: 3 Minutes

---

## 0:00 - 0:30 | The Hook & Introduction
*(Start on the Verdict Room dashboard. The empty nodes for "dinner" and "wine" are floating. The Evidence & Claims feed is empty.)*

**Speaker:**
> "Have you ever tried splitting a massive group tab after a chaotic night out? Everyone remembers it differently. 'I paid for this, you paid for that.' It’s a mess."
> 
> "Meet **Tab Split**. It’s an AI-powered truth engine. Instead of arguing in a group chat, you throw your claims and receipts into Tab Split, and it builds a real-time knowledge graph to mathematically determine the truth."

*(Point to the Cognee Proof panel in the bottom left).*
**Speaker:**
> "And this isn't just local state. We are backed by Cognee Cloud. You can see our data is successfully materializing to a remote graph right now."

---

## 0:30 - 1:30 | The Dispute (Data Entry & Graph Action)
*(Type into the Claim input on the left panel)*

**Speaker:**
> "Let’s look at last night. Alice says she paid the $250 dinner bill at Ocean Prime."

**Action:**
1. Enter Claim: `"I paid the $250 dinner bill at Ocean Prime."` (Topic: `dinner`, User: `Alice`)
2. *Click Submit.*

**Speaker:**
> "Watch the center graph. Tab Split immediately parses the entities and drops a 'dinner' centroid onto the canvas."

*(Type into the Claim input again)*

**Speaker:**
> "But Bob disputes this."

**Action:**
3. Enter Claim: `"Alice only paid for her own steak."` (Topic: `dinner`, User: `Bob`)
4. *Click Submit.*

**Speaker:**
> "The graph updates. Now we have conflicting claims orbiting the 'dinner' centroid. Look at the right panel—our Truth Centroid gauge for 'dinner' is hovering around 50%. The engine is uncertain."

---

## 1:30 - 2:30 | The Evidence (Resolution)
*(Switch to the Evidence Upload tab)*

**Speaker:**
> "Talk is cheap. Let’s upload some hard evidence."

**Action:**
5. Go to Evidence Upload.
6. Enter text: `"TOTAL: $250.00 VISA ending in 1234 (Alice)"`
7. Select Type: `Receipt`, Topic: `dinner`.
8. *Click Upload.*

**Speaker:**
> "Watch the physics engine. The receipt enters the graph and creates a strong structural link to Alice's claim. 
> Because Cognee's graph architecture correlates the receipt's metadata directly to her initial statement, the engine recognizes the truth."

*(Point to the Right Panel Verdict)*

**Speaker:**
> "Our Truth Centroid just spiked to 85% confidence. The leading verdict is definitively: Alice paid the $250 dinner bill."

---

## 2:30 - 3:00 | Shareability & Wrap-up
*(Hover over the Verdict Panel)*

**Speaker:**
> "Now that we have a confident resolution, the group chat needs to see it."

**Action:**
9. Click the **"Get the Receipt"** button that just appeared.
10. *The modal pops up showing the stylized neon receipt.*

**Speaker:**
> "Tab Split automatically generates a neon Vegas-style 'Verdict Receipt'. I can instantly copy this to my clipboard or download it as a high-res image and drop it straight into the group chat to end the argument once and for all."

*(Click Download PNG to demonstrate)*

**Speaker:**
> "With Cognee Cloud powering the memory and Tab Split analyzing the claims, the truth is no longer subjective. Thank you."

*(End Demo)*

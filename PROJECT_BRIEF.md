# Tab Split - Where's My Context?

## The Pitch
"Tab Split" is a real-time consensus engine for the morning after. When a group of friends wakes up with fragmented memories of a wild night, they submit conflicting claims and chaotic evidence (receipts, texts, blurry photos). Tab Split ingests this data, cross-references it using a Cognee-powered memory graph, and continuously converges on the most probable version of events. 
Users interact in "The Verdict Room", a live force-directed graph that dynamically reorganizes as new evidence shifts the balance of truth, culminating in a shareable neon "verdict receipt" of who owes what.

## Judging Criteria Targeting
- **Potential Impact**: Resolves universal, low-stakes disputes (who owes what) in a highly relatable and viral way, demonstrating how AI can mediate human disagreements.
- **Creativity & Innovation**: Flips the standard "chat with your data" paradigm into a multiplayer evidence-gathering game where the AI acts as a real-time detective and judge.
- **Technical Excellence**: Implements a dual-service architecture with a Next.js frontend and a FastAPI backend, utilizing WebSockets for live, dynamic force-graph updates.
- **Best Use of Cognee**: Showcases Cognee's dynamic knowledge graph capabilities by constantly updating memory structures and reranking facts as new context is introduced.
- **User Experience**: Delivers a highly visual, animated experience with a side-by-side view of the conceptual graph and the actual Cognee Cloud dashboard, proving the AI is working in real time.
- **Presentation Quality**: Culminates in a punchy, neon-styled shareable asset ("verdict receipt") perfect for a demo finale.
